# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 4, 2026

Thank you for using the Sambung Kata Discord Bot ("the Bot"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand exactly what data we collect and how it is used. This Privacy Policy explains our data collection practices to comply with Discord's Developer Terms of Service.

## 1. Data We Collect
The Bot only collects the minimum amount of data required for its core features (Trap System, PostCode System, Satpam Voice, and Booster Tracking) to function properly. 

We collect and store the following Discord data in our secure, private database:
- **Server (Guild) IDs**: To save server-specific configurations (e.g., trap channel locations, designated voice channels).
- **Channel IDs & Message IDs**: To keep track of automated interface panels so they can be refreshed automatically when the bot restarts.
- **User IDs**: To track the number of "strikes" a user has in the Trap System, to process temporary bans, and to keep track of user interactions with the PostCode redeeming system.

## 2. What We Do NOT Collect
- **Message Content**: We do not read, log, or store the contents of your messages. (Note: The Trap System instantly deletes messages sent in trap channels to protect the server, but the contents of those messages are never stored).
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect names, emails, IPs, or any other personally identifiable information outside of the public Discord User IDs.

## 3. How Data is Used
The collected data is used **strictly** to:
- Enforce the automated server protection features (Trap System).
- Manage and maintain your customized bot configurations across restarts.
- Prevent duplicate code redemptions in the PostCode system.

We do **not** sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion
Your data is stored for as long as it is necessary to provide our services. 
If you remove the Bot from your server, you may request the deletion of your server's stored configuration data. If you are a user who wishes to have your User ID wiped from our database (e.g., trap strikes), you may contact the Bot developers to request manual deletion.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or data deletion requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via Discord.
